UI & Features Revamp

12th of March, 2025

The entire website has now just been revamped! as you can see, everything looks more fresh and heck, more modern! I'll let you explore that on your own. Now onto the features!

Now, first of all, I added a contact me form on the main page, so you guys can send me emails from this very website! I also added a newsletter feed under it, you just plop your email there and BOOM!, you're subscribed!

Secondly, the blog post "jump to tags" function has also been changed. As you can see above the big blog titles, there's a small line of text that contains the blog number and the blog date. The jump to tags now function using the blog number. The format is #blog<#> with <#> being the blog number. For example, to automatically jump to this blog post, you would go to https://3rror-dev.github.io/blogs#blog9, note the main part: #blog9. Also, to view the latest blog post, you can now go to the tag #blog0!
